About us

It's a shout, not a whisper!

QYKSonic wants everyone to feel confident about the skin they’re in. Our mission is to beautifully transform skin and it does that by cleansing skin 5X better than hands alone. Gentle enough for twice daily use!

QYKSonic is headquartered in sunny Irvine, California, USA.

We’re not your average beauty company. from day one we made it our mission to cut the b.s. and create a brand that combats the idea that beauty is shallow. sure, we like to get dolled up and admire our foxy selves in the mirror, but that doesn’t mean we’re self-obsessed or conceited. we just know that when we look good, we feel great. and who wouldn’t want that?

QYK Sonic exists to bring you rad products, straightforward benefits, and daring content to help extract your best. there’s no shame in an awe-worthy complexion or head-turning hair. why? because taking care of your exceptional self means a bold, more confident you in this crazy world. we’re here to be an unapologetic shout in a crowd of conformity. after all, being the beautiful babe you are should never be boring.
